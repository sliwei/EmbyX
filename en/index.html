About

📱 EmbyX

v1.1

👋 Hi, I am @Juneix. EmbyX is a TikTok-style web player for Emby / Jellyfin. Experience your private media library in a whole new, immersive way. 🎉

All data stays on your device. Free to use.

🔮 Playback

  • Native HTML5 player, best on modern devices.
  • Older devices can use server transcoding.
